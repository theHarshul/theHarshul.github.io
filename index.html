Projects

Tandem Parking

Funded by the USC Startup Incubator, we are the Airbnb of Parking. We are seeking to solve parking scarcity in LA through pairing drivers looking for parking with lenders who have unused parking spots. I am leading the Engineering Team and building the Android App. Some cool & useful technologies we have used include RxJava, Retrofit, RetroLambda, Google Maps/Location, and Google SmartLock.

Community

A firebase-based, slack-like chat based on gelocation for community's to message and chat with each other and create their own channels invite others and view past history on chats in a simple interface. Also integrated login through Facebook,Google, or creating your own custom account. This app could be used to notify people in your community of any immediate dangers or can be used for community get togethers.

Plug

Inspired by my time at Sonos, I wanted to create an app that creates truly personal custom playlists. Music streaming services like Spotify, Apple Music, and etc. require you to follow a bunch of artists in order to understand your music taste. I attempted to streamline this process by analyzing metadata from your most personal source of music, the music library.

Ultra-Chess-1987

Chess with a Twist. A couple friends and I took the typical game of chess and made it a customized chess game with numerous modes including pong mode-playing a game of pong concurrently,timer mode, and customized pieces and placement mode. Using Java sockets, we made it a real-time gaming experience. My primary role on the project was with networking communication and interacting with mySQL to retain user info, game history, high scores and chess notation for each game.
